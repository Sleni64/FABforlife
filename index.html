B Street Pub House

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

7/10 for B Street – Good Vibes, Some Room to Grow B Street gets a respectable 7 out of 10 from me. The vibes are great—always lively, solid music, and it’s a fun spot to go with friends whether you're grabbing a bite or just hanging out. The food? Honestly, pretty good. Some items hit harder than others, but overall it’s satisfying. The fries and sandwiches are usually a win, and the drink selection holds its own. Service is friendly, though it can be a little hit or miss depending on how busy it is. What’s keeping it from being higher? Just a few small things—maybe a little more consistency in the food, maybe a stronger signature item that really makes B Street pop. But it’s still a place I’d recommend and come back to. Reliable, chill, and a good time. B Street is doing its thing.

Aca Taco

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

10 Big Booms for Aca Taco – Chico's Flavor King If I could give Aca Taco 10 Big Booms, I would without hesitation. This place delivers every single time. Whether you're rolling through for a quick burrito, diving into a giant plate of nachos, or living your best life with some tacos on the patio, it's always fire. 🌮🔥 The portions? Massive. The flavor? Chef’s kiss. And the salsa bar? A gift to humanity. It's the kind of place that makes you forget you're a college student running on fumes and caffeine—until you’re in a food coma, and it’s worth every bite. Service is chill, food comes out quick, and the vibe is very “post-class treat yourself” energy. A Chico classic.

Celestinos

💥💥💥💥💥💥

10 Big Booms for Celestino’s – Good Slice, But Not My Ride-or-Die Celestino’s gets 6 Big Booms from me. It’s solid. It’s reliable. But it’s not blowing my mind. The pizza slices are big, greasy in the best way, and hit the spot especially after a long day (or night 👀). The crust is decent, toppings are fine, and they don’t skimp on the cheese. But it can be inconsistent—some days the slice is fire, other days it's kinda meh and lukewarm. The vibe is very grab-and-go, and the staff gets you in and out quick. Great for a late-night craving or casual bite, but not necessarily where I’d go for a top-tier pizza experience. If you’re in the mood for New York-style and don’t feel like waiting, Celestino’s will do the job—but it’s not a full 10-Boom flavor explosion.

Unwind Kitchen and Bar

💥💥💥💥💥

Unwind Kitchen – Fresh, Flavorful, and a Chill Spot to Recharge Unwind Kitchen is a great place to grab a healthy, satisfying meal without sacrificing flavor. Their menu offers a variety of fresh bowls, salads, and wraps packed with wholesome ingredients that hit the spot whether you’re looking for something light or filling. The staff is friendly and attentive, and the atmosphere has a relaxed, modern vibe that makes it perfect for a quick lunch break or casual hangout. The food is consistently fresh, with vibrant flavors and great portion sizes. It’s clear they put care into sourcing quality ingredients and crafting balanced meals. If you want a nutritious, tasty option in a chill setting, Unwind Kitchen is definitely worth checking out.

Strong Water

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

Chico’s hidden gem for craft beer. If you’re searching for a place in Chico that blends the quality of a craft beer bar with the comfort and heart of a local eatery, The Lost Dutchman Taproom is it. It may have “taproom” in the name, but don’t let that fool you—this is just as much a full-on restaurant as it is a beer spot, and it delivers a full dining experience that feels thoughtful, satisfying, and welcoming from start to finish. One of the top customers (Hector) of this restaurant has been giving this spot a 10/10 for years, he said since he first went there, the vibes and food were immaculate and could never stop going back since!

Woodstock Pizza

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

9/10 Big Booms for Woodstock Pizza – A Slice Above the Rest Woodstock Pizza deserves a solid 9 out of 10 Big Booms—and for good reason! From the moment you take your first bite, you can tell this pizza is crafted with care and serious flavor. The crust strikes that perfect balance: crispy on the outside, soft and chewy inside. The toppings are fresh and generously layered, and the cheese melts just right without being greasy. Whether you’re into classic combos or their specialty pies, Woodstock consistently delivers. The vibe is casual and welcoming, great for hanging out with friends or grabbing a quick, delicious meal. Service is friendly and efficient, which just adds to the overall great experience. If you’re craving a quality pizza that feels homemade but better, Woodstock is your go-to. Just one point shy of perfection — mainly because I can never decide which amazing slice to pick!

PROJECT TEA

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

Big Booms for Project Tea – Almost Perfect! Project Tea in Chico is a go-to spot for a refreshing drink and a fun snack. The vibe is super chill, the drinks are creative and tasty (big fan of the boba options!), and the service is usually friendly and quick. They’ve earned 8 Big Booms from me 💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥—because they almost have it all. The only thing holding them back from a perfect 10 is consistency with the Korean corn dogs. Sometimes they hit just right—crispy, cheesy, golden perfection—and other times, they’re a little underwhelming or not available at all. If they can tighten up the corn dog game, this place would be a 10/10 no question. Still, highly recommend stopping by when you’re in the area!

Mondos Coffeehouse

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

10 Big Booms for Mondo’s Coffee – Pure Chico Magic Mondo’s Coffee? 10 BIG BOOMS, no question. This place is everything a coffee shop should be—cozy, welcoming, and packed with heart. The coffee is consistently top-tier (shoutout to the cold brew), and the bagel breakfast sandwiches? Unreal. Jalapeño cheddar, loaded eggy goodness, melty cheese—it’s the breakfast hug you didn’t know you needed. But what really sets Mondo’s apart is the vibe. It's not just about coffee—it's about community. Everyone is friendly, the space feels like a second home, and the mission behind it (supporting people with developmental disabilities) makes supporting them feel even better. Whether you're pulling up to study, hang with a friend, or just grab a quick bite, Mondo’s always delivers. 10/10 Big Booms. Mondo’s is the moment. 💥

Dutch bros

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

10/10 Big Booms for Dutch Bros! If I could give Dutch Bros TEN big booms, I would—because that’s exactly how much I love this place! Dutch Bros, you’ve officially earned 10 BIG BOOMS from me. 💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥 Keep being awesome.

Starbucks

💥💥💥

10 Big Booms for Starbucks – Basic Brew Energy Starbucks gets 3 Big Booms from me, and that’s mostly for being open and having bathrooms. Let’s be real—if coffee had a corporate dress code, this would be it. The drinks are either watered down or loaded with sugar to the point you forget you ordered coffee. Every seasonal drink tastes like a candle, and somehow still costs $7. Cool. Mobile order? Might as well send a message in a bottle. And if you’ve ever gotten a drink that actually looks like the menu photo, congrats—you’re the chosen one. Starbucks is the place you go when you don’t feel like making decisions or when you’ve given up trying to find something better. It’s not terrible... but it’s not giving anything more than “default caffeine source.”

The Boba Stop

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

The boba stop- Cozy, welcoming, grab & go If you’re in Chico and craving something refreshing, flavorful, and just a little fun, The Boba Stop should definitely be on your radar. Nestled just a few minutes from the Chico State campus, this small but vibrant shop delivers big on taste, creativity, and charm. Whether you’re a boba enthusiast or just looking for a sweet afternoon pick-me-up, this spot checks all the boxes. The drink menu at The Boba Stop is surprisingly diverse for such a small shop, offering everything from traditional milk teas to inventive snow bubbles and matcha blends. I started off with their Tiger Milk Tea, which features creamy milk tea layered with rich brown sugar syrup and chewy, fresh boba pearls. It’s sweet—but not overpoweringly so—with a smooth black tea base that brings balance. It’s the kind of drink that reminds you why boba took off in the first place. One of their everyday customers, Hanzel gave this boba spot a whopping 10/10 stars!! Hanzel:

Wing Stop

💥💥💥💥

Wingstop – Finger-Lickin’ Good, But Not Perfect Wingstop definitely knows how to satisfy a wing craving. The wings come out hot, crispy, and tossed in a variety of bold sauces that pack a punch—whether you’re all about the classic Original Hot or want to try something sweeter like Honey BBQ. The flavors are consistently tasty, and the portions are generous, making it great for sharing with friends or just treating yourself. The sides, especially the seasoned fries, are a nice bonus that round out the meal. That said, sometimes the service can be hit or miss, and depending on the location, wait times might get a bit long during busy hours. Overall, Wingstop is a reliable spot for solid wings and bold flavors—definitely earns some Big Booms from me!

Tacobell

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

Taco Bell – Fast, Flavorful, and Perfect for Late Nights Taco Bell is that reliable go-to when you want something quick, affordable, and tasty with a little kick. Their menu is full of creative combos and crave-worthy items—from crunchy tacos to cheesy burritos and those legendary Crunchwrap Supremes. The flavors are bold and satisfying, even if it’s not exactly gourmet food. Plus, they’re always rolling out fun limited-time items that keep things interesting. Service is usually fast, and the drive-thru is perfect for those late-night cravings or when you just want to treat yourself without breaking the bank. It might not be fine dining, but Taco Bell hits the spot every time. Definitely Big Boom worthy for a casual, flavorful bite!

Canes

💥💥💥💥💥

Raising Cane’s – Simple, Crispy, and Always Satisfying If you’re a fan of chicken fingers done right, Raising Cane’s is where it’s at. Their menu keeps it simple—just chicken fingers, fries, Texas toast, and their famous Cane’s sauce—but they do every item exceptionally well. The chicken fingers are juicy, crispy, and perfectly seasoned. The fries are crispy and fresh, and that Cane’s sauce? Legendary. It’s the perfect combo for dipping and keeps you coming back for more. Service is usually quick and friendly, and the atmosphere is casual and welcoming—great for a quick lunch or casual dinner with friends. If you want a no-fuss, delicious chicken finger fix, Raising Cane’s is a solid choice. Definitely earns some Big Booms for consistency and flavor!

Jersey Mikes

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

Jersey Mike’s – Classic Subs Done Right Jersey Mike’s serves up hearty, fresh subs that hit the spot every time. The bread is soft yet sturdy enough to hold all those delicious meats and fresh veggies without falling apart. Whether you’re a fan of their famous “Mike’s Way” with onions, peppers, and their special dressing or prefer to build your own, the flavors always come through. The meats and cheeses taste high-quality and the portion sizes are generous, making it a great value for a quick, satisfying meal. The staff is usually friendly and quick, and the shops have that classic sub shop vibe that feels welcoming. If you’re craving a solid sandwich with fresh ingredients and no fuss, Jersey Mike’s is a dependable go-to.

Panda Express

💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥💥

Panda Express – Fast, Familiar, and Comfortingly Predictable Panda Express is the classic go-to for quick Chinese-American food that’s consistent and convenient. Their dishes are flavorful, though they lean more on the sweet and savory side than authentic. Favorites like Orange Chicken and Beijing Beef are crowd-pleasers and deliver that addictive balance of crispy, saucy, and satisfying. The portion sizes are generous, and the service is usually fast, making it perfect for a quick lunch or dinner when you want something warm and filling without the wait. While it’s not the place for a truly authentic Chinese meal, Panda Express hits the spot when you’re craving bold flavors and easy comfort food.
